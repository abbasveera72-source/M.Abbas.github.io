# My First Experience with Object-Oriented Programming at UET

During my second semester at **UET Lahore Faisalabad Campus**, I was introduced to **Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)** for the first time. Initially, learning OOP was quite challenging because concepts such as classes, objects, inheritance, and polymorphism were completely new to me.

At the beginning of the semester, I found it difficult to understand how different OOP concepts worked together. Writing programs and debugging errors often consumed a significant amount of time. However, as the semester progressed, I gradually started enjoying programming and became more interested in software development.

A major factor that helped me overcome these difficulties was the guidance provided by **Dr. Bilal Ahmad**. He continuously supported and motivated us throughout the semester. His teaching style made complex concepts easier to understand and inspired me to improve my programming skills.

One of the most memorable experiences of this semester was working on different academic projects. In OOP, we developed a **Game** and a **University Management System**. These projects strengthened my understanding of programming concepts and improved my problem-solving abilities.

In addition, our **Database Management System (DBMS)** project on the **Lung Cancer Risk Prediction System** introduced us to the exciting field of Machine Learning collaboration. This experience helped me understand how software and intelligent systems can be used to solve real-world problems.

Another memorable event during the semester was participating in the **project exhibitions** organized for OOP and DBMS projects. These exhibitions allowed us to present our work, learn from other students, and gain valuable practical experience.

Apart from academics, I maintained a balanced routine by studying approximately **five hours daily** while also enjoying my free time with friends. University life is not only about studies; it is also about creating unforgettable memories. Many experiences from this semester have become golden memories that I will always cherish.

Overall, my first experience with Object-Oriented Programming transformed my perspective toward programming. What initially seemed difficult eventually became one of the most enjoyable and rewarding learning experiences of my academic journey.
